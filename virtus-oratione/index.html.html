Schedule Your 24-Hour Review
The most valuable part of this exercise happens tomorrow. After 24 hours, you will have enough distance from the recording to hear and see yourself with fresh ears and eyes. We will remind you to complete three review steps:
I
Read your transcript. Notice your word choices, your sentence structures, your filler words. Mark what you like and dislike before you ever hear your voice.
II
Listen without picture. Audio only. Close your eyes. What does your voice actually sound like to others? Notice tone, pace, confidence, variety.
III
Watch without sound. Mute it entirely. What story does your body tell? Posture, gestures, facial expression, eye contact — all visible now without distraction.

Observations — Voice
My voice sounds confident and grounded
My pace is varied — I don't sound like I'm rushing
My tone changes meaningfully with the content
I notice uptalk — my statements rising like questions
My volume feels projected and present — not meek or strained
There are long pauses or "um/ah" moments I want to eliminate
Your notes on your voice
"Watch yourself with the sound completely off. You cannot be distracted by words now. What does your body communicate? What story does your presence tell?"
Silent Video — Watch Your Body

The video is muted. Watch your posture, your hands, your facial expressions, your eye contact. Imagine you are watching a stranger. What impression do they give?

I make direct eye contact with the camera / audience
